Sidney Royel Selby III (born May 3, 1997), better known by his stage name Desiigner, is an American hip hop recording artist from Brooklyn, New York. He rose to prominence when his debut single "Panda", reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart. In February 2016, fellow American rapper Kanye West (who sampled "Panda" for his seventh album The Life of Pablo), announced Desiigner's signing to his GOOD Music imprint, under the aegis of Def Jam Recordings.

Contents

Early life

Sidney Royel Selby III was born on May 3, 1997, in the Bedford–Stuyvesant neighborhood of Brooklyn, New York. He began practicing vocals in the school choir and at church. After getting shot at the age of 14, he began to embark on a music career and dropped out of high school.

Career

Desiigner embarked on his rapping career under his original moniker Dezolo. He later began working on his tracks under the alias of Designer Royel, as Royel is his middle name. It was his sister who convinced him to drop the middle name and add the extra I. Under his new pseudonym, Desiigner released his official debut track, produced by The Shell God and titled "Zombie Walk", on December 3, 2015. On December 15, 2015, his commercial debut single, titled "Panda", was released on YouTube and SoundCloud, and on iTunes just five days later before being re-released in February 2016.

On February 11, 2016, Desiigner signed a recording contract to GOOD Music, under the aegis of Def Jam Recordings. After signing a record deal, Desiigner was subsequently featured on the two tracks, "Pt. 2" (which features an interpolation of "Panda") and "Freestyle 4" performed by Kanye West. These two tracks were included on West's seventh album The Life of Pablo. Desiigner later performed his newest song, "Pluto", at the 2016's South by Southwest (SXSW) music festival. In an interview with Billboard, Desiigner confirmed the release of his upcoming trap mixtape, titled Trap History Month.

On May 5, 2016, American record producer Mike Dean, announced that he would be executive producing Desiigner's debut studio album. On May 24, 2016, Desiigner announced the title of his debut album to be The Life of Desiigner. In June, XXL magazine revealed their annual "Freshmen Class" for 2016, which included Desiigner, along with other nine new artists. In June, Desiigner made his US television debut, when he performed his single "Panda", at the 2016 BET Awards.

On June 26, 2016, Desiigner released his first full-length project, a mixtape titled New English, on Tidal, following a live premiere of the mixtape on June 22.

Musical style

Desiigner's musical style is influenced by southern trap music. His rapping technique and vocals have been compared to that of Atlanta-based rapper Future. When asked about the frequent comparisons, he told Complex: "God gave him a blessing, but he gave me a blessing too. I ain't gonna doubt the man's music. He make beautiful music too. Music is made every day. Big ups to him, big ups to Future. I actually like Future's music. I like his music, you feel me. I’m not a hater or a critic on him, you know, I do me. God bless him, God bless me. But don't get mad because I took a style and made it better than his. Technically, we all out here trying to get money. Do you. Not to sound funny but maybe one day that man could learn something from me-feel me?"
